POGOH Bike Sharing

Overview

With over 60 stations and 600 bikes across 25 neighborhoods, POGOHis Pittsburgh's bikeshare program. The system aims to provide a convenient, affordable, and sustainable transportation option for residents that is alternative to cars.

Problem

Despite POGOH's potential benefits for the city as well as its people, the bike-sharing system faces challenges related to widespread user adoption and experience.

Approach

I analyzed quantitative and geographic data from 27,417 trips taken during March 2024 using Python to make inferences about patterns across a larger user sample, and conducted semi-structured interviews with 14 residents to uncover pain points and motivations.
